About me

Welcome to Tan Li En's Photography!

Welcome to my website! I'm Tanlien, a 20-year-old photographer who fell in love with the art of capturing moments during my secondary school years. With over 7 years of experience under my belt, I have honed my skills and developed a deep appreciation for the magic of black and white photography.

For me, black and white photography is a gateway to a world of timeless beauty. Through the absence of color, I focus on capturing the essence of a sceneâ€”the interplay of light and shadow, the intricate details, and the emotions that unfold. I believe that black and white photography has a unique ability to evoke a sense of nostalgia, depth, and raw emotion.

By skillfully utilizing the contrast between black and white, I create images that bring out the true character and essence of the subject. Through the careful manipulation of shapes, textures, and atmosphere, I aim to evoke a profound visual and emotional experience for viewers. Black and white photography allows me to express not only the physical aspects but also the psychological and emotional nuances of the subjects I capture.
